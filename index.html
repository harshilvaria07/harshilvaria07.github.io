About

Who I am & how I work

I combine hands-on security tools with clear communication so teams know what to fix, why it matters, and how to prevent it in future.

As a Associate Security Operations Specialist with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering and more than two years of experience in securing networks, applications and cloud environments.
